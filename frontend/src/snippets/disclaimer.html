openMediAid is NOT a diagnostic tool capable of providing irrefutable diagnoses or treatments. It is NOT meant to replace doctor consultations but supplement the entire medical decision process with useful information for both patients and doctors.
All personal medical data is stored ANONYMOUSLY, meaning that no references to the actual identity of its owner is held anywhere in the system. This does not guarantee, however, that reconstruction of identity is in all cases impossible.
